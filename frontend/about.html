About HeartGuard

HeartGuard is an AI-powered cardiovascular health platform that helps you predict, monitor, and improve your heart health through intelligent analysis and community support.

Health First

Every feature is designed with your cardiovascular wellbeing in mind

Privacy

Your health data stays on your device. No cloud storage, no tracking

AI-Powered

Advanced machine learning models for accurate risk prediction

Community

Anonymous support network for shared health journeys

Our Mission

To democratize heart health monitoring and make AI-driven cardiovascular risk prediction accessible to everyone. Early detection saves lives, and HeartGuard exists to make that support practical, private, and approachable.
