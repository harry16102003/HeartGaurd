Quick Emergency Steps

  1. Call emergency services immediately if symptoms are severe.
  2. Sit or lie down in a safe place and stay as calm as possible.
  3. Unlock your phone and keep it nearby for responders or family.
  4. If advised by your doctor, take your prescribed emergency medication.
